IncidentChanger

Turn incident information into a safer workplace

A fully web- and mobile based application that allows your team to record, qualify and categorize incidents, and define, prioritize and track follow-up actions. The mobile app helps to record incidents or anomalies on the spot while collecting hot data in the form of image/video, audio and GPS coordinates.
